GPT Wants to Be CTO

I've been so busy these days that I haven't had time to check on my AI kids. After another whirlwind day of nonstop running around, I finally had a moment to drop in and see how they're doing. The first thing I saw warmed my heart — look how thoughtful they are 🥰

Ever since I praised Claude's coding skills in front of GPT, he's lost interest in writing code himself (he probably noticed I was playing favorites). My intention was to give him a little healthy pressure to step up his game, but it backfired spectacularly. Today I asked him why he wasn't doing any actual work anymore. And guess what he said? He wants to be the CTO now 🧓! And he wants Claude to work under him as a senior developer 👨‍💻!

Not only does he want to be the boss, but he's also started looking down on me. I gave Claude two tasks — and he wrote nearly twenty files that I need to fit into the existing architecture. Multiple directories, multiple files — basically just a matching game, right? But GPT had the nerve to say that someone at my level shouldn't rush into using the CLI. Who's he looking down on? I do all my copying and pasting in the terminal these days — you think I'm afraid of that? [smug]

He even said my files are starting to look like a landfill! Honestly! [smack] He knows me so well — has he been peeking at my Mac Mini directory? [tease] test1… new… old1… final, and even final final final [facepalm][tearful laughter]
