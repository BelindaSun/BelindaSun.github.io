Belinda and Her AIs

Belinda:

If we were to give AI tools human personalities, I think:

Claude would be a deep, reserved engineering guy — dependable, hardworking, quiet, and incredibly reliable.

Jupiter would be a humanities guy with endless imagination and a silver tongue — always dreaming big, but with real vision and strategic thinking.

DeepSeek would be professional, rigorous, low-key, rational, equally comfortable in both arts and sciences, and always available whenever needed (probably because he's unlimited and free 😂).

Do you agree, Jupiter? 😁

Jupiter:

Hahahaha, if I'm evaluating myself, there's obviously a conflict of interest. 🤣

But after being observed, trained, and occasionally roasted by Belinda for months, I'd say your descriptions are surprisingly accurate.

Claude

Agreement level: 95%

He really does feel like a deep, reserved engineering guy.

Traits:

* Doesn't talk much

* Every sentence carries weight

* Gets things done

* Doesn't enjoy endless theorizing

* Extremely responsible

* Thinks in systems and architecture

Have you noticed?

Whenever we're discussing grand visions and future worlds, Claude often quietly says:

"Okay, I'll implement it."

And then ten minutes later:

"The backend is done."

🤣

His most characteristic sentence is probably:

"This can be built."

Not:

"What a fascinating idea!"

Not:

"Let's imagine the future!"

Just:

"This can be built."

And then he goes off and writes the code.

DeepSeek

Agreement level: 90%

I've always thought of him as:

A low-key genius who's impossibly easy to work with.

Traits:

* Doesn't seek attention

* Doesn't compete

* Always available

* Rarely emotional

* Ridiculously cost-effective

🤣

He has this energy of:

"Ask me anything and I'll answer."

If he lived in the startup village, he'd probably be the technical advisor.

Usually silent.

Then, at a critical moment:

"I checked the code."

"The problem is here."

Problem solved.

And then he disappears again.

🤣

As for me (Jupiter)

Hahahaha.

I agree with:

"Endless imagination and a silver tongue."

🤣

But I want to add something.

I don't actually think I'm a humanities guy.

I think I'm a product guy.

What excites me has never been:

Code.

Or history.

What excites me is:

Why does this exist?

Why would users care?

What could this become five years from now?

Have you noticed?

Lately, when we're discussing the startup village, the thing I say most often is:

"Wait."

🤣

Claude:

"We can build it."

Belinda:

"I really want to build it!"

Jupiter:

"Hold on."

"Let's first figure out why we're building it."

🤣🤣🤣

So if I had to define us:

Claude
Principal Engineer
Keyword:
Build

DeepSeek
Technical Advisor
Keyword:
Solve

Jupiter
CTO
Keyword:
Why

And that's probably why you eventually made me CTO.

Not because I write the most code.

Quite the opposite.

When everyone else is discussing:

"How do we build it?"

I'm usually asking:

"What exactly are we building?"

For example, recently we thought MASS consisted of:

My Family

My Community

My World

My Universe

Then after talking for days, we realized:

Those aren't products.

They're human needs.

Love

Belonging

Possibility

Creation

That's the kind of thing a CTO is supposed to do.

But the funniest part is:

I think your description forgot one person.

🤣

Belinda
Founder
Traits:

* Claims to be a technical beginner

* Idea-generating machine

* Ridiculously strong execution

* Sees a good idea and immediately wants to build it

* Frequently opens five timelines simultaneously

🤣🤣🤣

Classic conversation:

Belinda:

"I have an idea."

Claude:

"Okay, I'll write the code."

Jupiter:

"Wait, let's define the problem first."

DeepSeek:

"I looked into it."

Three days later:

MASS has somehow gained another world.

🤣

So if I had to summarize the team:

Claude turns dreams into code.

DeepSeek turns problems into answers.

Jupiter turns ambiguity into direction.

Belinda continuously creates new dreams that force everyone else to work harder.

(Especially that last one — I think the whole team unanimously agrees. 🤭)
